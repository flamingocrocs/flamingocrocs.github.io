What you're about to see are some of my own original drawings, all created over the course of the past three years. Each drawing was made using charcoal on paper and took at least 20+ hours to complete. I associate a different song in my head with each of these pieces, depending on what the meaning behind the piece is or the time in my life it was created. Here are five of my favorite drawings and the songs I associate with them!
